The problem

Nanopayments are impossible today.

Creators lose 8–12%

Stripe 2.9% + $0.30. Patreon 8–12%. Multi-day holds. KYC + biz docs.

AI agents can't use those rails

Credit cards need humans. ACH is days. API-key buckets don't settle.

Traditional gas kills tiny invoices

Ethereum L1: $0.50–$5/tx. An agent making $0.001 calls buries revenue in gas.

No unified primitive

Tips, subs, paywalls, API billing each need their own stack + their own login.

The batchPay trick

1 transaction → 10 discrete $0.001 calls.

// Agent prepays 10 inference credits — one on-chain tx, ~$0.01 total
await arcpay.api.batchPay('alice', 'ai-fe', 10);

// Each subsequent call is just a wallet signature, verified on-chain
await arcpay.api.call('alice', 'ai-fe', prompt);

Our contract shows 6 on-chain txs → 60 logical calls. The Arcscan page carries a "Logs 50+" badge on 6 transactions. That compression is the economic unlock.
